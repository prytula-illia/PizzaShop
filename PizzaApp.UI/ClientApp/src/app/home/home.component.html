Hello, this is pizza shop application!

Here you can order our pizza or create a recepie of a new one!

To order pizza:

  • Click 'Pizza menu' button on navigation menu.
  • Use the scroll to move throuhg menu.
  • To order pizza click on the 'Order pizza' button.
  • You will be navigated to payments page were you can view a time needed for your pizza cooking.

To make your own pizza recepie:

  • Click 'Make pizza' button on navigation menu.
  • Enter pizza name.
  • Add or remove ingridients using + or - buttons.
  • When you finished click 'Create pizza recepie' button on the bottom of the page. New pizza will be added to the menu.
  • *Some of the ingridients may be missing. To book more ingridients you should manage the storage page.

For managing storage:

  • Click 'Storage' button on navigation menu.
  • Navigate the mouse on needed ingridient.
  • To book 100 more ingridient click on the 'Book more' button.
